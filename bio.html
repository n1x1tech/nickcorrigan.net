The feeling I get when the lights go out and the show is about to begin is the reason I chose live entertainment as my profession. I savor the chance to create experiences that are larger than life for both the artist and the audience.
I am a multimedia specialist and an audio video professional with nearly 20 years of experience in all aspects of live production technology. I currently work for Radford University as a Multimedia Designer and AI research and designer. I help develop learning content and create AI tools. It use technology to bring people together and communicate effectively. Utilizing production technology in live environments is one of my passions and has been throughout my career.
My first love is audio. My interest in live performances began when I played in bands in high school and throughout college at the University of Tennessee. Playing music progressed to mixing live bands and live broadcasts of bands and into a career as a professional audio visual technician. I am certified in many aspects of AV systems integration and operation.
